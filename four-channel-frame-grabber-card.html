Four Channel Frame Grabber Card

4-Channel Frame Grabber card concurrently captures four channels of Analog NTSC/PAL video and four channels of stereo audio. Base board captures raw video frames from each channel at up to full frame rate—30 frames/s for NTSC and 25 frames/s for PAL. Four BNC connectors on the mounting bracket can be used to connect external composite video sources. Four 3.5mm audio jack connector connects Line-in stereo audio inputs. Two RS422 ports- one for user data and another one for GUI parameters handshaking. The unit supports VGA, DVI-D display ports; this unit supports NTSC video captures frame rates up to 30 frames per second (fps) at an aspect ratio of 720x480.supports PAL video capture frame rates up to 25 fps and a 720x576 aspect ratio. Audio can be stereo or mono; the sampling rate supports 8 kHz to 96 kHz. The high frame-video capture and stream out either through a single-lane x1 PCIe interface or Gigabit LAN port which makes it suitable for the board for applications such as broadcast-grade video/audio capturing. This unit got one POE Gigabit port to support video surveillance, law enforcement, and traffic monitoring. LED indication for video Capture rate.
