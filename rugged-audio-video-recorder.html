Rugged Audio/Video Recorder

Rugged Audio Video Recorder module is designed to capture and store DVI-I Video-In and Line-In audio signals in an interleaved fashion. It has a Video Bypass DVI-I Output port compatible to BARCO Display.

Rugged Audio/Video Recorder Features and Applications

Features

  • TI DaVinci Video Processors DM8148 Form Factor-6U VME.
  • Configurable Video frame rate 25 to 60 frames/sec.
  • Supports video bit rates 100 to 1000Kbits/Second.
  • Audio channel configurable to stereo or mono input/output.
  • Supports WAV and PCM audio encoding.
  • Time stamping and bookmark facility for recorded data.
  • Gigabit Ethernet interfaces.
  • Supports video VESA Modes from 640x480 to 1920x1200 configurable , 24 bit true colors.
  • Capture Rate: Capture rates of 4Fps to 30Fps for resolution up to 1920x1200 Pixels, programmable, 24 bit true colors.
  • Resolution : 24bit true colors.
  • Encoding : Video Codec: H.264, MPEG-4 (AVC) Configurable.
  • Dimensions : 6U VME card with single slot width.
  • Weight : 750 gms.
  • Operating Temp : -20°C TO +55°C.
  • Storage Temp : -40°C TO +85°C.
  • Note: MIL-STD-810D for Air bone devices Severity limits as per usage inside a transport class aircraft cabin.

Applications

  • Video inputs and Outputs - Number of inputs : 1 minimum.
  • Video format : DVI and VGA format.
  • Resolution : Programmable for max 1920x1200 pixels.
  • RADAR
