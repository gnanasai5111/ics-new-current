Innovation Communications Systems

Innovation Communications Systems Ltd. (ICS) is a technology-driven organization with a strong focus on Communication product development and services.

Established in year 1988, ICS started its journey under the commendable leadership of a group of technocrats and has witnessed sustained growth in the business.

Our business is based on a customer centric approach built on a strong ethical foundation.

The major clients of the ICS include leading cellular/basic Telecom operators, public sector organizations and defence labs.

Our Product Portfolio includes Design and development of Communication Systems like SATCOM modems, Custom modems, High Capacity Radio Relay—IRR-B4 (HCRR) , Software Defined Radio (SDR) – IVUR and Digital Mobile Radio (DMR) - LCR 400.
