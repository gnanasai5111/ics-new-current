ISO 9001 – 2015

ICS obtained ISO 9001:2000 certification in 2001 and subsequently upgraded to ISO 9001:2008 standard in 2009 and ISO 9001:2015 standard in 2017 for Design, Development, Manufacture and Supply of Telecommunications , IT related equipment Software Development in Communications and Information Technology.

AS9100D

ICS group company has been certified for AS9100D for Design and Manufacturing of Electronics and Communication Systems for Defence and Aerospace applications.

DSIR R & D Recognition

ICS R&D Unit is recognized by Department of Scientific & Industrial Research, Government of India, New Delhi as an In-House R&D unit.

Cemilac Approval, Corporate Membership

ICS obtained ISO 9001 - 2000 certification in October 2001 from "American Quality Assessors" for ICS is a Registered Member of the Electronics and Software Export Promotion Council, Government of India , FAPCCI : Federation of Andhra Pradesh Chambers of Commerce and Industry and ELIAP - Electronic Industries Association of Andhra Pradesh

Fapcci Award

Mr. B. K. Murti, Executive Director receiving the FAPCCI Award from Dr. YS Rajasekhara Reddy Chief Minister Andhra Pradesh. The award has received in recognition of their R & D efforts for developing SDR (Software Defined Radio) Technology required for Defence of India.
